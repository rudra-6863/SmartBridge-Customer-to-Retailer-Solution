RO Water (Reverse Osmosis Water) - Description Reverse Osmosis (RO) water is purified water that has been filtered using a reverse osmosis system. This process removes impurities, contaminants, and dissolved solids, making the water safe, clean, and better tasting.

Benefits of RO Water:

    1. Removes up to 99% of contaminants (bacteria, viruses, chemicals, chlorine, etc.)
    2. Better taste and odor-free compared to tap water
    3. Safe for drinking & cooking
    4. Reduces health risks related to contaminated water
    5. Ideal for homes, offices, and industries
